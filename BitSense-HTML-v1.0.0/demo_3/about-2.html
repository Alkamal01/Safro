About BitSense

  • Home

Established in 2013 as the first cloud mining provider, BitSense has become a multi-functional cryptocurrency exchange, trusted by over a million users. BitSense offers cross-platform trading via website, mobile app, WebSocket and REST API, providing access to high liquidity orderbook for top currency pairs on the market. Instant Bitcoin buying and selling is available via simplified bundle interface.
