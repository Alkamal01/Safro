Introducing our new

Wallet Application

The BitSense Wallet mobile application for iOS and Android is the key element of the BitSense trading ecosystem. Our wallet app makes it easy for you to trade currencies and cryptocurrencies, with complete support for margin trading. You can buy and sell assets such as bitcoin and ether on the open-source BitSense Exchange, our next-generation trading platform, with immediate settlement and direct ownership, thanks to distributed ledger technology.

How to use

BitSense Wallet

  • Download

    It’s very easy to download your free Bitsense Wallet app. You can download it for either iOS or Android.

  • Register

    Register for a free and 100% secure account on Bitsense Wallet using our application.

  • Introduce

    Provide a photo, ID and proof of address. The app guides you through the process. It takes just five minutes to complete.

  • Add Funds

    Put some money into your Wallet: Deposit bitcoins, transfer funds from a bank card or e-wallet, or make a wire transfer.

  • Begin trading

    Buy and sell fiat currencies as well as cryptocurrencies, or invest in ICOs, all without fees.
